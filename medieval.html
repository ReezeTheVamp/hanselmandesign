final presentation

With Medieval Skyscrapers, I pushed my knowledge of foliage, terrain, and lighting in Unreal Engine while improving my understanding of modular environment design. I think the final environment is a bit inorganic, but I'm happy with my progress.

All presented assets are my own - meshes, materials, particles, lighting, etc. withthe exception of the beautiful music by Peter Gundry.
