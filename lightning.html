final presentation

My goal for this project was to improve my understanding of materials, particles, and sequencer within the Unreal Engine. I chose to create a weapon I had been conceptualizing for Hamendall, the lightning pistol - somewhat familiar ground.

The work included creating a rigged and animated mesh, particle effects, several materials, and audio effects. All of this came together in UE Sequencer, which controls the assets and material transition.
